Story
In a time before humanity, Anubis, the god of the dead, ruled with unmatched power. His dominion extended beyond the Nile, encompassing realms seen and unseen. Controlling elements and traversing between life and death, he summoned souls for his Eternal Legion.

Despite envy and ambition among his followers, particularly cunning jackals, Anubis maintained the balance between life and death, his legacy enduring through the ages.
Under the guidance of their shrewd chieftains, the wolves devised a plan to ensnare Anubis using ancient and arcane rituals. Deceived by those he once guided, he was ensconced within a concealed sepulcher for ages.

Yet, Anubis has awoken, driven by a singular quest for retribution against his betrayers and to restore his sovereignty. None can evade the ferocity of his sinister sorcery and the relentless march of his Eternal Guard.
